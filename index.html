Nootropics for the Rationalist

Which parts of the nervous system are useful to tweak for enhanced cognitive performance? Here are some promising receptor types:

Serotonin

Many receptor subtypes. Increased level of the natural agonist of the same name generally increases cognitive activity.

Dopamine

Involved in motivation and motor control. Results of tampering are powerful and can be dangerous as well as useful.

Opiod

Involved in reward and sensation. Useful and dangerous.

Alpha, Beta

Often overlooked, several subtypes. Involved in short-term physical performance and can be targeted with antagonists to counteract sometimes-undesirable effects, like elevated blood pressure and heart rate, that may be caused by other substances.

AMPA

Racetams act as agonist. Involved in learning and memory, also seizures. Fortunately most racetams are anticonvulsants.

GABA

Main receptor types A and B. Well known for relaxation / anxiolytic effects and related dependence. Nootropic potential under review.

nAChRs

Nicotine acts as agonist. Useful for stimulation. Other nootropic potential under review.
